DiceCTF 2026 Quals

Início: 07 March 2026 - 17:00 UTC

Localzação: Online

Organizadores: DiceGang

Descrição: Play DiceCTF 2026 Quals for a chance to qualify for DiceCTF Finals! DiceCTF 2026 Finals will have 12 teams participating in total. 8 teams will be from the Open Division, whilst 4 teams will be from the US & Canada Division. To qualify for the US & Canada Division, your team must consist entirely of players from the US or Canada, as well as placing within the top 4 teams in this division. To qualify for the Open Division, your team must place within the top 8 Open Division teams. If a team who is eligible for the US & Canada Division places within the top 8 teams overall within the CTF, the place will go to the next open division team who have not already qualified. There are no age restrictions on finalists who can attend. However, residents of Cuba, Iran, Syria, North Korea, or Crimea are unable to attend due to export controls and sanction programs. Additionally, sanctioned individuals are unable to attend DiceCTF 2026 Finals. Thanks to Margin Research, Zellic, and OtterSec for sponsoring. Join our Discord server: https://discord.gg/dicectf
